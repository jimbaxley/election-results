# Design System Document

## Theme Overview

This document outlines the core visual characteristics of our design system.

## Color Palette

Our brand utilizes a vibrant color palette to establish a distinct and engaging user experience.

- **Primary Color**: `#233C7E` - A deep, authoritative blue, used for primary calls to action, prominent headings, and key brand elements.
- **Secondary Color**: `#A6266E` - A rich, warm magenta, providing a complementary accent for secondary buttons, interactive elements, and visual highlights.
- **Tertiary Color**: `#2499FF` - A bright, energetic sky blue, serving as an additional accent for notifications, badges, and interactive states.
- **Neutral Color**: `#797777` - A balanced medium gray, used for backgrounds, borders, and supportive text, ensuring readability and visual comfort.

The system is configured for a `light` color mode, optimizing for bright interfaces and readability.

## Typography

Our typographic system is built for clarity and modern appeal.

- **Headline Font**: `Public Sans` - Chosen for its strong presence and versatility, ideal for headlines and titles.
- **Body Font**: `Public Sans` - A highly readable sans-serif, perfect for extended text and general UI elements.
- **Label Font**: `Public Sans` - Consistent with body text for form labels and smaller annotations, ensuring uniformity.

## Shape and Spacing

- **Roundedness**: `2` (Moderate) - Elements feature a subtle degree of roundedness, providing a friendly yet professional aesthetic without being overly soft or sharp.
- **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - The layout maintains a comfortable and balanced amount of whitespace, ensuring elements breathe and information is easily digestible.
